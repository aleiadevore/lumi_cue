Features of Lumi Cue

Lumi Cue offers a different experience setting timers in your life. Set the mood to focus by simply invoking Alexa to open the Lumi Cue skill. Our timer works with your Lifx Bulb to provide a visual timer.

  • Create custom timers for work, school, and home.
  • Seamlessly transition from a neutral to a focused state and back with Lumi Cue's lighting. Your light will transition between calming colors for the exact length of your timer, returning to its original state when the timer is complete.
